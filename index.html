Lofsdalen is a calm, family friendly mountain village close to Stockholm/Arlanda. Here is everything you could wish for in active and memorable holiday in the mountains. Untouched magnificent nature, combined with well maintained facilities for both summer and winter activities makes Lofsdalen to an attractive mountain destination. In summertime you can go for a hike in the mountains, rent a bike, rent a boat, go fishing and take a refreshing swim in the lake. In wintertime you can enjoy skiing and Lofsdalen offers 6-seat chairlifts that will take you up the mountains.

Two cozy houses with beach location and wonderful view over the lake and the mountains, both available in summer and wintertime. In summertime you can go the short distance to the beach and take a refreshing swim in the lake and make a bonfire. Do you like to go by boat? In summertime a rowing boat is available for free if you rent a cabin. It is also possible to book a new boat with a 6-horse four-stroke engine. In wintertime its perfect to go with scooter at the lake where the trail goes. Here is the place if you want to relax from everyday life and just enjoy. The smaller house has a sauna and the bigger house has a fireplace, you decide which best fits your needs! The houses have the best location with 1 km from grocery shop ICA, restaurants and bike rental etc. The houses are located two kilometers close to the ski resort, it takes 5 minutes to go there by car.

Activities


Bicycle Rental Edens Garden in Svartådalen is an excellent starting point for a bike ride. Experience the area with your sences; see, feel, smell, hear and taste the mixture of nature's ingredients. A combination of charming paved roads and gravel roads takes you to picturesque small towns such as Fläckebo, Sätra Brunn, Haraker sawmill and beautifull Svanå manor. Side by side, with the river 'Svartån' in focus, you cycle through a beautiful Swedish historical and cultural landscape. Under the heading 'Our Specials' you will find an attractive short break bike package with a lovely spa experience.
