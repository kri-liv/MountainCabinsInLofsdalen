Beaversafari Did you know that the beaver is a keystone species to nature and helps to hold ecosystems together? As introduction, your guide John de Jong, inform you passionated about the beaver and the wetlands, at the same time you are served a 'fika' with coffee cooked over an open fire. During the evening, as the wind usually calms down and the water becomes still, we paddle together quietly and carefully the canoe over the river 'Svartån' into the beaver realm. A symphony of birdsongs, trumpet from cranes... And there suddenly it rattles in the reeds! Searching for beavers in 'Svartådalen' is really an exiting experience.

Canoeing The Black River Valley ('Svartådalen'), in the region of Västmanland, is a smile in this agricultural landscape. It is well known for it's valuable bird-rich wetlands (Natura2000) and its key biotopes with a fascinating biodiversity. Svartådalen contains a very beautiful and calm lake system that is joined by the winding river 'Svartån'. The Black River Valley is thus excellent for discrete exploring via canoe. Edens Garden offers canoe rental under the heading 'Activities', and you will find attractive package deals in the heading 'Our Specials'.

Bicycle Rental Edens Garden in Svartådalen is an excellent starting point for a bike ride. Experience the area with your sences; see, feel, smell, hear and taste the mixture of nature's ingredients. A combination of charming paved roads and gravel roads takes you to picturesque small towns such as Fläckebo, Sätra Brunn, Haraker sawmill and beautifull Svanå manor. Side by side, with the river 'Svartån' in focus, you cycle through a beautiful Swedish historical and cultural landscape. Under the heading 'Our Specials' you will find an attractive short break bike package with a lovely spa experience.
